What's Binomial distribution?

binomial distribution measures the chance of : how likely i will get a specific number of successes (that has it's own chance) after this many tries?

e.g: a coin flip, we first set our success , either the head will be our success or the tail. let's go with the head witch has a successes chance(p) of 50% to happen, now we start throwing the coin (n) number of times , 5 times. after that, we got head(the setted success) 3 times... what are odds of such?

And this is where Binomial distribution comes into play, to know what are the odds of getting 3 heads You can go ahead and press the button below and search for 3, to find out its ... 31% chance! and the best part is we can see the chance of the other numbers too! even the chance of getting no heads (0).

Binomial graph

we don't have to limit ourselves to the coin example, we can set the number of tries(n) and the chance of the success(p) to output different results! the more you play with this graph the better you will understand binomial distribution.
